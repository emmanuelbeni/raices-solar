Our Mission

Caribbean farmers help feed the world, yet many still live with unreliable electricity, limited access to technology, and little support when disasters strike.

Raíces Solar works to change that through renewable energy, farm innovation, and community advocacy.

Our Pillars

☀️ Energy Access

Solar installations for farms, homes, and rural communities.

🌱 Farm Technology

Irrigation systems, sensors, cold storage, and precision agriculture.

📢 Advocacy

Supporting farmers facing displacement and land-rights challenges.
